Our Story

Born from a Passion
for the Heights

Founded in 2009 by IFMGA-certified guide Marco Steinhardt, Alpenglow Expeditions grew from a single rope team on the Matterhorn into one of the most trusted mountain guiding companies in the world.

We believe the mountains offer more than adrenaline — they offer clarity, perspective, and a profound connection to the natural world. Every expedition we lead is designed to honour that.

  • IFMGA / UIAGM certified guide team
  • Expeditions on 6 continents, 40+ mountain ranges
  • Partnerships with local mountain rescue organisations
  • Leave No Trace committed — zero environmental compromise
